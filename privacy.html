Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Duxedo is a student-focused platform that provides customisable, printable engineering graphics sheets. We are operated by Duxedo's owner only at the moment.Contact: hello@duxedo.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Duxedo:

  • Account Information: Your full name, email address or phone number, and password (stored locally in your browser via localStorage).
  • Sheet Details: Title, name, class/branch, roll number, sheet number, academic year, starting date, and submission date — provided after payment for sheet generation.
  • Payment Information: Your UTR / Transaction Reference Number and selected payment method. We do not store your card numbers or UPI PIN.
  • Reviews: Name, college/year, star rating, and review text if you choose to submit a review.
  • Usage Data: All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not use external analytics or tracking cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your ₹10 payment and unlock your sheet download.
  • To pre-fill your sheet with the details you provide.
  • To maintain purchase records visible in our admin panel.
  • To display reviews you submit on the public reviews page.
  • To respond to your support requests.

4. Data Storage

All user data on Duxedo is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device to a third-party server.
  • Data is not shared across devices or browsers.

5. Payment Data

Payments are processed via UPI to Duxedo does not process card payments through a gateway. UTR numbers submitted are stored locally for admin verification purposes only.

6. Children's Privacy

Duxedo is designed for engineering college students (typically 18+ years of age). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us at our official E-Mail

7. Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of any reviews you submitted by contacting at our official E-Mail
  • You may request a delition of your purchase records by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of Duxedo after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related concerns, email us at hello@duxedo.com
