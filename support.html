Support

Last updated: June 2026

📧 Contact Us

Have a question or issue? Reach us at hello.duxedo@gmail.com — we typically respond within 6–8 hours on weekdays.

For payment-related issues, please have your Order ID and UTR number ready when you contact us.

🛒 How to Purchase a Sheet

  1. Visit Browse Sheets and click ₹10 – Buy & Download on any sheet you want.
  2. On the checkout page, choose your preferred payment method: Scan QR (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), UPI ID, Card, or Net Banking.
  3. Pay exactly ₹10
  4. Copy the UTR / Transaction Reference Number from your UPI app and enter it on the checkout page.
  5. After verification, a form will appear — fill in your sheet details (name, roll number, class, sheet number, dates).
  6. Click Generate & Download Sheet to get your print-ready PDF within a span.

🔒 Payment Security

All payments are processed directly via UPI to the registered merchant UPI ID. Duxedo does not store your card or banking credentials. UTR verification is done manually by our team and may result into delay of service during heavy business hours and/or days.

↩️ Refund Policy

We offer refunds in the following cases:

  • If payment was deducted but the sheet was not delivered within 24 hours.
  • If a technical error on our end prevented download.
  • Duplicate payments for the same sheet.

To request a refund, email with your Order ID and UTR within 7 days of purchase.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My payment went through but I didn't get the sheet form?
A: Contact support with your payment proof and details.
Q: Can I use the sheet for multiple submissions?
A: Each ₹10 purchase covers one sheet download. You can fill in different details each time you purchase.
Q: What format is the download?
A: All sheets are downloaded as print-ready A3 PDF files, formatted to standard engineering lab requirements.
Q: I entered the wrong details in the sheet form. Can I redo it?
A: Please contact us at our official E-Mail ID with your Order ID and we'll issue a re-download credit at no extra charge.
